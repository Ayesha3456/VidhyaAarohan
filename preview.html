A structured, student-friendly approach that builds real understanding — not just exam scores.

✔️ Builds lasting understanding✔️ No rote memorisation✔️ Foundation-first

Visual Examples & Diagrams

✔️ Diagrams for geometry✔️ Number lines✔️ Visual word problems

Math Puzzles & Quizzes

Fun quizzes and logic puzzles at the end of each session make learning enjoyable and help assess understanding without the pressure of formal tests.

Practice Worksheets

Structured worksheets aligned with CBSE syllabus are shared after each session. Practice at home reinforces what was taught during the live class.

✔️ Topic-wise✔️ Graded difficulty✔️ CBSE-aligned

Revision & Doubt Clearing

✔️ Regular revision✔️ Open doubt-clearing✔️ Exam prep support
Our Philosophy

Math is a subject to enjoy, not fear

At AMR Digital Academy, we believe every student has the potential to be good at math. The difference is in the teaching. We focus on curiosity, patience, and progressive challenge — ensuring students feel confident walking into every exam.

Our small batch size of 5–6 students allows the teacher to observe each student's pace and tailor explanations accordingly. There's no "one-size-fits-all" in our classroom.

Founder's Note

Two Passions, One Mission

AMR Digital Academy was founded with a simple belief: every child can be good at math with the right guidance and a teacher who genuinely cares.

As someone with a postgraduate degree in Applied Mathematics and hands-on experience as an Angular developer, I have always found joy in both teaching and building. Whether it's helping a Class 4 student finally understand fractions, or building a clean, responsive business website — the feeling of creating something useful drives everything I do.

I started this academy to offer affordable, high-quality online math tuition in small batches where every student is seen and heard. Large online classes often leave students behind — that's exactly what we fix with our 5–6 student limit.

Alongside the academy, I offer freelance web development services using Angular, Python, Django, and modern front-end tools — helping small businesses and professionals create a strong online presence.
