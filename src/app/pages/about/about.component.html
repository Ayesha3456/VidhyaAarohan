Founder's Note

Two Passions, One Mission

AMR Digital Academy was founded with a simple belief: every child can be good at math with the right guidance and a teacher who genuinely cares.

As someone with a postgraduate degree in Applied Mathematics and hands-on experience as an Angular developer, I have always found joy in both teaching and building. Whether it's helping a Class 4 student finally understand fractions, or building a clean, responsive business website — the feeling of creating something useful drives everything I do.

I started this academy to offer affordable, high-quality online math tuition in small batches where every student is seen and heard. Large online classes often leave students behind — that's exactly what we fix with our 5–6 student limit.

Alongside the academy, I offer freelance web development services using Angular, Python, Django, and modern front-end tools — helping small businesses and professionals create a strong online presence.
