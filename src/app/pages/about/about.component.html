Founder’s Note

Focused Teaching. Real Results.

I started AMR Digital Academy with a simple goal — to make math easier and more understandable for students from Classes 1 to 12.

With a postgraduate degree in Applied Mathematics, I focus on building strong concepts instead of rote learning. Every class is designed to help students actually understand what they are learning.

Classes are conducted in small batches (5–6 students) so that every child gets personal attention, can ask questions freely, and improve with confidence.

I also work on web development projects for businesses, but my primary focus is helping students improve in mathematics through structured teaching.
